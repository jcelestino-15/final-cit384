About Me

Family Dog

Yogi was born on October 7, 2010. He was adopted into my family on August 14, 2014. He is a chihuahua mix breed. His name was inspired by the cartoon character, Yogi Bear.

His favorite snack is Milk Bone Flavor Snacks Biscuit Dog Treats. He loves banana and watermelon. He likes to play with my cousin's dog, Juno. He loves to stay in bed with his blue blanket.

He is a confident and loyal dog. He likes to play with other people.
