Tobi Celestino

About Me

Family Dog

Tobi was born on January 2, 2018. Tobi was adopted on December 26, 2020. He is a chihuahua mix breed. His name was inspired by my brother's favorite character, Obito Uchiha, from an anime called Naruto; Tobi is the name of Obito's alter-ego.

His favorite snack is Jerky Treats Tender Beef Strips. His favorite hobby is sleeping.

He is a timid dog. He likes to be petted and cuddled. He likes to take long walks around the neighborhood.
